Engines

Eight natural sound engines

Each engine generates a different class of natural sound. Layer and blend them to build environments.

Wind

Filtered noise with spectral shaping, gust modulation, and variable tilt.

Water

Flow and turbulence modeling with resonant body size and ripple physics.

Birds

Synthesized calls with pitch variation, chirp speed, and flock density.

Insects

Swarm synthesis with buzz frequency, pulse rate, and chorus detuning.

Thunder

Storm modeling with rumble depth, crack transients, and distance simulation.

Rain

Stochastic drop generation with variable density, size, surface, and splash.

Aurora

Harmonic drone layers with slow drift, shimmer modulation, and brightness.

Ice

Cracking and resonant stress modeling with thickness and decay control.

Evolution

Organic, autonomous change

Parameters shift continuously with configurable rate, density, complexity, warmth, and drift. Engines gradually transform over time, creating soundscapes that never loop.

Eight engines — live simulation
Macros

Hands-on performance control

Four assignable macros, a learn-to-route workflow, and an XY pad for performing two macros at once. Build custom controls on top of the engines, then play them.

Four Macros

Each macro can drive any number of parameters simultaneously, with per-destination depth and polarity.

Learn Mode

Arm a macro, then touch any slider anywhere in the plugin — the next gesture becomes a route.

XY Pad

Pick any two macros as X and Y, then perform them with one gesture. Every route follows.

XM1 YM2

One gesture, a dozen parameters

  • Arm a macro by clicking Learn — an amber indicator confirms.
  • Move any slider in any panel to bind it to the armed macro.
  • Tap chips in the macro panel to remove routes; shift-click to invert polarity.
  • Assign two macros to the XY pad and play the entire instrument in a single gesture.
