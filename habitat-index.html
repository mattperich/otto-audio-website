Habitat

Generative drone synthesizer. Eight engines modeled on the natural world. macOS, iPad, AU & VST3.

GitHub

Eight natural sound engines

Each engine generates a different class of natural sound. Layer and blend them to build environments.

Wind

Filtered noise with spectral shaping, gust modulation, and variable tilt.

Water

Flow and turbulence modeling with resonant body size and ripple physics.

Birds

Synthesized calls with pitch variation, chirp speed, and flock density.

Insects

Swarm synthesis with buzz frequency, pulse rate, and chorus detuning.

Thunder

Storm modeling with rumble depth, crack transients, and distance simulation.

Rain

Stochastic drop generation with variable density, size, surface, and splash.

Aurora

Harmonic drone layers with slow drift, shimmer modulation, and brightness.

Ice

Cracking and resonant stress modeling with thickness and decay control.

Organic evolution

Autonomous parameter evolution with configurable rate, density, complexity, warmth, and drift. Engines gradually shift over time, creating slowly transforming soundscapes without manual input.

Studio-quality effects

Delay, reverb, and character shaping applied to the combined engine output.

Delay

Flange, chorus, and delay ranges with digital, analog, and tape character modes.

Reverb

18 algorithmic presets spanning plate, hall, cathedral, shimmer, and infinite wash.

Character

Drive, compression, and tone shaping at the output stage.
