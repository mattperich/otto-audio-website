About

Built for musicians, by a musician

Otto Audio is the work of Matt Perich — building tools that treat audio software as an instrument in its own right. Every plugin is designed around real-time interaction, visual feedback, and deep sonic control.

All apps run natively on macOS and iPad as AU and VST3 plugins, with standalone versions for exploration and performance.
